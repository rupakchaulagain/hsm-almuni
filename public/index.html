About HSMSS Alumni

Hetauda School of Management Alumni Association (HSMAA) conceptualizes the potential bondage among the alumni can only ensure the academic and professional growth simultaneously. Thus, we are trying to create a circle that comprises all the bachelor and master graduates from this academia residing wheresoever within the nation or abroad. The only motto of HSMAA is to connect you and fellow graduates encompassing network opportunities for their professional and academic success, programmes, voluntary services and many more. So you all are appealed to stay tuned with HSMAA since the union in HSMAA is always for the welfare of its Alumni.
